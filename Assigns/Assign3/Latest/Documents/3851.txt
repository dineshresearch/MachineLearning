List of countries by external debt
This is a list of countries by gross (i.e. total) external debt.
"External debt" is defined as the total public and private debt owed to nonresidents repayable in foreign currency, goods, or services (see definition). The list is based on The World Factbook unless otherwise noted. Source [1] accessed on October 2008. For informational purposes several non-sovereign entities are also included in this list.
