List of meteor showers


This list of meteor streams and peak activity times is based on data from the International Meteor Organization while most of the parent body associations are from Gary Kronk's book, Meteor Showers': A Description Catalog, Enslow Publishers, New Jersey, ISBN 0-89490-071-4, and from Peter Jennisken's book, "Meteor Showers and Their Parent Comets", Cambridge University Press, Cambridge UK, ISBN 13-978-0-521-85349-1.
