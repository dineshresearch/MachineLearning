List of American Dad! episodes
The following is a list of episodes for the FOX animated television series American Dad!. The show premiered on February 6, 2005 in the post Super Bowl slot. The series began regular airings on May 1, 2005.
It is currently airing its 4th season, which also includes part of a 22 episode fourth production cycle of episodes[1] FOX has also renewed American Dad for a fifth production cycle of 22 episodes to begin airing during 5th Season. [2]


While the first season is only 19 episodes production-wise, episodes from the second season were added to it since the season started in May, much earlier than is common for a television series.


As with most shows, the 3rd season had a reduced number of episodes due to the 2007-2008 Writers Guild of America strike.
